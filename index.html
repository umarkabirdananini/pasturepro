MISSION & VISION

Our mission is to bridge the livestock feed resource gap by delivering premium-quality feed, advanced equipment, and expert services, empowering farmers to achieve sustainable and efficient operations.

Our vision is to become the leading provider of livestock feed solutions in Nigeria and beyond, renowned for product quality, sustainable practices, and innovative approaches that drive a thriving livestock sector.

STRUCTURE


PasturePro is organized into five core units: Sales, Consultancy, Training, Services, and Production. Each unit is strategically designed to meet specific needs, whether by offering high-quality feed products, providing expert guidance, or delivering specialized training and resources to livestock farmers. 

INTEGRATED APPROACH

Our integrated approach combines feed production, consulting, training, and equipment supply to provide farmers with a comprehensive support system. This holistic strategy enables farmers to address every aspect of livestock feed and pasture management, helping them maximize productivity and sustainability.

BRIDGING THE GAP

PasturePro was founded to address the gap between feed supply and demand in the livestock sector. Through continuous innovation and dedication, we are bridging this gap by offering accessible, high-quality solutions that empower farmers at every level.

BUSINESS

PRODUCTION

At PasturePro, we produce top-quality feed products designed to meet the nutritional needs of livestock. Our production processes prioritize quality, sustainability, and efficiency, ensuring that our products help farmers achieve optimal livestock health and performance. 

SALE

Our Sales team is dedicated to making our feed products readily available across Nigeria. We offer a variety of feed solutions, including hay, forage, silage, concentrates, and supplements, designed to meet the unique needs of livestock farmers.

SERVICES

Beyond feed, PasturePro provides a range of services to support farmers, including equipment sales, maintenance, and specialized support services. Our goal is to ensure that farmers have the resources they need to succeed.

TRAINING

Our Training unit equips livestock farmers with the knowledge and skills needed for effective feed management, pasture care, and sustainable practices. Our workshops and courses are designed to promote best practices and innovative techniques in the livestock industry.

CONSULTANCY

PasturePro’s Consultancy services offer expert guidance to help farmers optimize their operations. From feed planning to pasture management, our consultants work closely with farmers to enhance productivity and long-term sustainability.

PARTNERS

OUR PARTNERS



We work with various stakeholders across the agricultural and livestock sectors, including suppliers, agricultural institutions, and community organizations. These partnerships allow us to expand our reach and enhance the value we provide to livestock farmers.




COMMUNITY ENGAGEMENT

PasturePro is committed to making a positive impact within the communities we serve. We support community projects, engage with local farmers, and work to create opportunities that uplift and empower rural and urban farming communities.

WORK WITH US


Are you passionate about transforming the livestock sector? Join us at PasturePro! We are always looking for dedicated professionals and partners to help us drive change and make a difference in livestock feed solutions.
